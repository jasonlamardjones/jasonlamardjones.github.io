Why This Work Matters Now

Cabo Verde digital-readiness context

Cabo Verde’s digital-economy landscape is becoming more visible through initiatives such as TechPark CV and Skodji Digital. My independent work does not represent these institutions, but it responds to the same practical challenge: helping students, young adults, and community members understand opportunities, prepare realistically, and take useful next steps through English, digital readiness, resource navigation, and follow up support.

The challenge is not only whether opportunities exist. The challenge is whether people can find them, understand them, trust them, and feel ready to act.
