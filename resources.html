Why this hub exists

This page was created by Jason L. Jones as an independent community-development resource to help students, workers, teachers, entrepreneurs, and community members find practical learning pathways without having to search dozens of separate websites.

The goal is to spotlight useful resources, connect people to existing organizations, and reduce the friction between discovering an opportunity and taking the first step.
