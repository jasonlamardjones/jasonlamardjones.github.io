Why this exists

This hub was created by Jason L. Jones, an independent volunteer, educator, and community-development strategist who came to Cabo Verde to learn, contribute, and explore practical ways to connect people with useful resources. The goal is simple: reduce the gap between people’s potential and the opportunities they know how to access.

This is independent from any official American Spaces, Embassy, school, company, or listed provider unless a specific partnership or event is clearly stated.
