Why I created this hub

I created this resource hub after spending time in Cabo Verde and seeing how many useful learning, career, language, digital-skills, entrepreneurship, and study resources exist but are scattered across many websites, social media pages, organizations, and temporary announcements.

Cabo Verde is the first pilot focus because this is where the work began. The hub is also designed to grow over time into a broader opportunity-access resource for online learners and communities in other locations.

The purpose is simple: make useful opportunities easier to find, easier to understand, and easier to act on. The hub is meant to help students, workers, teachers, entrepreneurs, managers, volunteers, and community members identify one realistic next step instead of feeling overwhelmed by disconnected information.

Who I am

My background includes organizational leadership, management, education support, veteran outreach, community outreach, English teaching, instructional design interests, and practical program support across different community needs. In Cabo Verde, my work has focused on listening, learning, mapping resources, supporting educational engagement, and exploring ways to connect people with practical opportunity pathways.

This hub is part of a broader professional proof of concept: resource curation, public-interest design, training support, community-development strategy, and opportunity mapping.

What this is — and what it is not

It is independently curated.
It is not officially sponsored, endorsed, or maintained by any listed organization unless clearly stated.
It is informational.
Users should always verify current deadlines, costs, certificates, and eligibility on the official provider website.
It is meant to complement existing work.
The goal is to spotlight useful resources and connect people to organizations already doing valuable work, not replace local actors.
It is a starting point.
A listing here does not guarantee quality, acceptance, employment, certification, scholarship, or official recognition.

How resources are selected

Resources are prioritized when they are useful, accessible, relevant to the current pilot focus or online learners, connected to learning or opportunity access, and available through a public source. Some entries are listed as “Verify Details” or “Watch Next Cycle” when users should check the official provider page before acting.

For local organizations, the goal is to use neutral listing language, invite corrections, and avoid implying endorsement, partnership, or official affiliation without permission.

Status labels used in the Resource Hub

Open Now means an opportunity appears currently available. Ongoing means it is usually available anytime or self-paced. Upcoming means a future date is visible. Watch Next Cycle means the opportunity may return but the current window may be closed. Verify Details or Check Official Site means users should check the official provider page before acting.
