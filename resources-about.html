About this hub

The Learning & Career Resource Hub was created by Jason L. Jones, an independent volunteer, educator, and community-development strategist who came to Cabo Verde to learn, contribute, and identify practical ways to connect people with useful resources.

The purpose is to help students, workers, teachers, managers, entrepreneurs, and community members discover training, language-learning tools, digital-skills resources, business support, career pathways, and selected opportunities that may otherwise be difficult to find or understand.

Why it matters

Many people do not lack potential. They lack exposure, guidance, networks, confidence, digital access, or a clear next step. This hub is designed to reduce that friction by organizing resources into practical pathways.

What it is not

This page is not an official Embassy, American Spaces, university, government, or provider page unless a specific item is clearly marked as official. Listings are informational and should be checked directly with the provider.

How it can grow

The hub can grow into multiple pages: open opportunities, island-specific resources, enrollment guides, provider profiles, video walkthroughs, and QR-code landing pages for hotels, schools, community groups, and workshops.

How to help

You can suggest a resource, help verify details, offer training or mentorship, share a local opportunity, or discuss responsible support for student and community access.
