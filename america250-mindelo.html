About the Series / Sobre a Série

This activity series runs through June and July at American Space Mindelo / UniCV. It connects the America250 theme — independence, opportunity, and the future — to the everyday goals of the people who take part.

Across short, practical sessions, participants reflect on where they are now, explore the opportunities available to them, practice English, engage with U.S. culture, and choose one realistic next step they can act on after the series.

Sessions are welcoming and beginner-friendly. You can join one workshop or follow the whole series. Exact time and room details are confirmed through registration updates and the QR schedule page.

Jun11

What Do I Want to Build?

O que quero construir?

This opening workshop helps participants reflect on independence, opportunity, and the future by connecting big ideas to their own goals, strengths, and next steps.

Objective

Help participants identify what they want to build in their life, education, career, community, or personal development.

What participants will do
  • Reflect on their goals, interests, and strengths.
  • Discuss what “opportunity” means in their own life.
  • Use a simple strategic futuring activity to imagine possible futures.
  • Choose one area they want to grow or build next.
What participants will walk away with
  • A clearer personal goal or direction.
  • One practical next step they can take after the workshop.
  • A stronger connection between the America250 theme and their own future.
  • A simple reflection they can use for future learning, study, or career planning.

Este workshop ajuda os participantes a refletir sobre os seus objetivos, pontos fortes e o futuro que desejam construir, escolhendo um próximo passo prático.
